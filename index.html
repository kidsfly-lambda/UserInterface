A Helping Hand

We've all been there, Junior is on his 15th tantrum. you have all of 10 minutes to get to your gate. And your finding it really hard to hold a kicking toddler and two suit cases. This is where Kids Fly comes in, simply select your estimated arrival and departure time, and one of our trained associates will meet you at the airport entrance and escort you to your gate, giving you the help you need to get you to your terminal on time and stress free.
